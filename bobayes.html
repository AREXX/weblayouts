Step 04 Why "yes"?

It started with a "Yes!" from our kid.

Bubble tea spots were popping up everywhere — but none of them really felt like family. We wanted a place where everyone is treated like loved ones: where you can enjoy your favorite drink, take in the smell of fresh snacks, and feel right at home.

So we said, "let's just do it." And that's how Boba-Yes was born.
